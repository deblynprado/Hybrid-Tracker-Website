Common Questions

Where is my data stored?

Your data stays on your device and your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your refueling records, locations, or vehicle details.

How do I sync between devices?

Make sure you are logged into the same iCloud account on both devices and that iCloud Drive is enabled for Hybrid Tracker in your system settings.

Is there a Pro version?

Yes, Hybrid Tracker Pro unlocks advanced reports, location mapping, and multi-vehicle support. You can find more details inside the app settings.
